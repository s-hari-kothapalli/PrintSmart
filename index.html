How It Works

Six-stage pipeline.
Real-time decisions.

From raw G-code to a firmware-level safety decision — all on-device, no cloud required, under 50ms latency.

KEY PRINCIPLE

"We turn raw machine instructions into actionable safety decisions."

01

G-Code Parsing

Raw toolpath instructions ingested in real time as the file streams to the printer.

02

Geometric Reconstruction

Toolpaths converted into a voxel/mesh approximation rebuilding the 3D intent.

03

Feature Extraction

Cylindrical bores, trigger-assembly patterns, and stress points detected.

04

ML Classification

Hybrid rules + ML classifier trained on allowed vs. prohibited geometries.

05

Risk Scoring

Nuanced scores replace blunt blocks — configurable thresholds per institution.

06

Firmware Kill-Switch

Edge inference triggers allow, flag, or halt at firmware level before a layer prints.

Ethics & Privacy

Safety and privacy
are not in conflict.

Built from first principles to protect both compliance and individual privacy — they reinforce each other.

01

Runs Entirely On-Device

All inference happens locally. No cloud dependency. Your G-code never leaves the machine — ever.

02

No Design Storage Required

PrintSmart does not store CAD files or G-code. Analysis is stateless — processed and discarded.

03

Institutional Compliance, Not Surveillance

Configured by institutions to enforce their own policies. We provide infrastructure — you set the rules.

04

Transparent Risk Scoring

Every decision is explainable and auditable. No black-box enforcement — ever.
